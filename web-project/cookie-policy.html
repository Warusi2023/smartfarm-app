How we use cookies and tracking technologies

Last Updated: August 25, 2025

1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how SmartFarm Inc. ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website, use our mobile applications, or interact with our services (collectively, the "Service").

By using our Service, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy. You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings and the options we provide.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, such as your preferred language and other settings, which can make your next visit easier and more useful.

2.1 Types of Cookies

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that are deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Cookies that remain on your device for a set period or until manually deleted
  • First-Party Cookies: Cookies set by our website
  • Third-Party Cookies: Cookies set by third-party services we use

3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several purposes to enhance your experience and improve our Service:

3.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the Service to function properly and cannot be disabled. They include:

  • Authentication and security cookies
  • Session management cookies
  • Load balancing and performance cookies

3.2 Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization:

  • Language and region preferences
  • User interface customization
  • Form auto-completion
  • Feature preferences and settings

3.3 Analytics and Performance Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our Service:

  • Page views and navigation patterns
  • Feature usage and performance metrics
  • Error tracking and debugging
  • User experience optimization

3.4 Marketing and Advertising Cookies

These cookies are used to deliver relevant advertisements and track marketing campaign effectiveness:

  • Ad targeting and personalization
  • Campaign performance measurement
  • Cross-site tracking for advertising
  • Social media integration

6. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

6.1 Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • View and delete existing cookies
  • Block cookies from specific websites
  • Block all cookies
  • Set preferences for different types of cookies

6.2 Our Cookie Consent Manager

We provide a cookie consent manager that allows you to:

  • Accept or reject non-essential cookies
  • Customize cookie preferences by category
  • Update preferences at any time
  • Withdraw consent for specific cookie types

6.3 Mobile Applications

For our mobile applications, you can manage tracking preferences through:

  • Device settings and privacy controls
  • App-specific privacy settings
  • Operating system privacy features

7. Impact of Disabling Cookies

Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our Service:

7.1 Essential Cookies

Disabling essential cookies will prevent the Service from functioning properly, including:

  • User authentication and login
  • Basic website functionality
  • Security features

7.2 Functional Cookies

Disabling functional cookies may result in:

  • Loss of personalized settings
  • Reduced user experience
  • Need to re-enter preferences

7.3 Analytics and Marketing Cookies

Disabling these cookies may affect:

  • Service improvement and optimization
  • Personalized content delivery
  • Relevant advertising

8. Do Not Track Signals

Some browsers include a "Do Not Track" (DNT) feature that sends a signal to websites indicating your preference not to be tracked. We respect DNT signals and will not track users who have enabled this feature for analytics or advertising purposes.

However, please note that DNT is not yet standardized, and different browsers may implement it differently. We recommend using our cookie consent manager for more precise control over tracking preferences.
