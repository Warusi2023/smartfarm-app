Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with SmartFarm?
Getting started is easy! Simply download our mobile app or visit our web platform, create an account, and follow our setup wizard. Our team will guide you through the initial configuration of your farm profile and sensors.
What kind of support do you offer?
We offer comprehensive support including 24/7 technical assistance, training sessions, documentation, video tutorials, and a community forum where farmers can share experiences and best practices.
Can I integrate SmartFarm with existing farm equipment?
Yes! SmartFarm is designed to work with most existing farm equipment. We provide integration guides and support for popular brands. Our open API also allows custom integrations for specialized equipment.
How accurate are the AI predictions?
Our AI models achieve 85-95% accuracy for yield predictions and disease detection. Accuracy improves over time as the system learns from your specific farm conditions and data patterns.
