Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started for free?

Click Start Free Trial to open registration, create your account, and verify your email. Then log in to use the dashboard.

How do I upgrade to Farm Pro?

Log in, open subscription management, and click Upgrade to Farm Pro. You will complete payment on Stripe's secure checkout page.

Can I pay online with a credit card?

Yes — Farm Pro uses Stripe Checkout. Enterprise remains contact-only.

Is there a setup fee?

Contact us for current Professional and Enterprise terms. Free registration does not require a credit card.

Do you offer annual billing?

Annual and custom billing may be available for paid plans. Contact our sales team for options.
