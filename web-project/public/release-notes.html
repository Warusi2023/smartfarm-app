SmartFarm Dashboard Release Notes

Dashboard reliability & weekly planning update

Official user-facing announcement

We've updated the SmartFarm dashboard to make it more reliable and easier to use. Your command center, weekly checklist, priorities, reset flow, and "This week's focus" now load more consistently and stay in sync with your farm data. The weather section is wired in and will show local conditions when your farm location and weather service are fully configured; until then you'll see a clear status message instead of broken or misleading data. If you notice anything unexpected, please let us know so we can improve it further.

A mobile-friendly version of this experience is also on the way, so you'll soon be able to use these improvements more comfortably from your phone and tablet.

What's included

  • Command center — daily checklist, weekly summary, and attention items
  • Weekly priorities — plan and track what matters this week
  • Weekly reset — carry forward or close out last week's work
  • This week's focus — progress pills for your top priorities
  • Weather — connected; shows local insights when location and service are configured
