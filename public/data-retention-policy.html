Data Retention & Deletion Policy

How long we keep your data and how to request deletion

Last Updated: January 2024

1. Introduction

This Data Retention and Deletion Policy explains how SmartFarm ("we," "our," or "us") retains and deletes your personal information. This policy should be read together with our Privacy Policy.

We are committed to retaining your data only for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations, while respecting your right to have your data deleted.

2. Data Retention Principles

We follow these principles when determining how long to retain your data:

  • Necessity: We retain data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in our Privacy Policy
  • Legal Compliance: We retain data as required by applicable laws and regulations
  • Service Provision: We retain data needed to provide and improve our services
  • User Rights: We respect your right to request deletion of your data
  • Security: We securely delete data when it is no longer needed

3. Data Retention Periods

The following table outlines how long we retain different types of data:

Data Type Retention Period Reason
Account Information While account is active + 3 years after closure Service provision, legal compliance, dispute resolution
Farm Data (crops, livestock, inventory) While account is active + 1 year after closure Service provision, data recovery
Financial Records 7 years after last transaction Legal requirement (tax, accounting)
Transaction Records 7 years after transaction Legal requirement (financial regulations)
Communication Records 3 years after last communication Customer support, legal compliance
Analytics Data 2 years Service improvement, aggregated statistics
Log Files 90 days Security, troubleshooting
Backup Data 30 days after deletion request Data recovery, disaster recovery
Marketing Data Until consent withdrawn + 1 year Marketing communications
Deleted Account Data 30 days after deletion request Recovery period, final backups

4. Factors Affecting Retention Periods

We may retain data longer than the standard periods in certain circumstances:

4.1 Legal Requirements

We may be required by law to retain certain data for specific periods, including:

  • Tax and accounting records (typically 7 years)
  • Financial transaction records (typically 7 years)
  • Legal proceedings and disputes (until resolved)
  • Regulatory compliance requirements

4.2 Active Disputes

If you have an ongoing dispute or claim, we may retain relevant data until the matter is resolved.

4.3 Legitimate Business Interests

We may retain data for legitimate business interests, such as:

  • Fraud prevention and detection
  • Security and safety
  • Service improvement
  • Historical analysis (in anonymized form)

5. Data Deletion

5.1 Your Right to Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data. You can request deletion by:

  • Using the account deletion feature in your account settings
  • Contacting us at privacy@smartfarm.com
  • Submitting a deletion request through our support system

5.2 What Gets Deleted

When you request deletion, we will delete:

  • Your account information
  • Your farm data (crops, livestock, inventory, etc.)
  • Your personal preferences and settings
  • Your communication history
  • Your analytics data

5.3 What May Be Retained

We may retain certain data even after deletion requests:

  • Legal Records: Financial and transaction records required by law
  • Anonymized Data: Aggregated, anonymized data that cannot identify you
  • Backup Data: Data in backups (deleted within 30 days)
  • Dispute Records: Data related to ongoing disputes or legal proceedings

5.4 Deletion Process

Our deletion process includes:

  1. Verification of your identity
  2. Review of data retention requirements
  3. Deletion of data from active systems
  4. Deletion of data from backups (within 30 days)
  5. Confirmation of deletion

6. Account Closure

6.1 Closing Your Account

You can close your account at any time through:

  • Account settings in the application
  • Contacting customer support
  • Emailing us at support@smartfarm.com

6.2 Data After Account Closure

After you close your account:

  • Your account will be deactivated immediately
  • Most data will be deleted within 30 days
  • Some data may be retained for legal compliance
  • You can request immediate deletion if preferred

6.3 Data Recovery

After account closure, you have 30 days to recover your data. After this period, data recovery may not be possible.

7. Data Anonymization

Instead of deleting data, we may anonymize it by:

  • Removing all personally identifiable information
  • Aggregating data with other users' data
  • Using data only for statistical and analytical purposes

Anonymized data cannot be used to identify you and is not subject to deletion requests.

8. Backup and Recovery

We maintain backups of your data for disaster recovery and business continuity. Backups are:

  • Retained for 30 days
  • Securely stored and encrypted
  • Deleted according to our retention schedule
  • Not accessible for routine data access

Data in backups will be deleted within 30 days of your deletion request.

9. Third-Party Data

We may share your data with third-party service providers. When you request deletion:

  • We will request deletion from third parties
  • Third parties may have their own retention policies
  • We cannot guarantee immediate deletion by third parties
  • We will confirm deletion with third parties

10. Exceptions to Deletion

We may not be able to delete your data if:

  • It is required by law or regulation
  • It is needed for ongoing legal proceedings
  • It is necessary for fraud prevention
  • It is part of a legitimate business interest
  • Deletion would harm other users' rights

If we cannot delete your data, we will explain why and inform you of your rights.

11. Data Export Before Deletion

Before deleting your data, you can:

  • Export your data in a portable format (JSON, CSV, PDF)
  • Download your farm records and reports
  • Save your financial data
  • Backup your important information

We recommend exporting your data before requesting deletion, as deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Data Retention and Deletion Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on our website
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending email notifications for significant changes
