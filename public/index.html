Frequently Asked Questions

What is SmartFarm?

SmartFarm is a comprehensive digital platform designed to help farmers and agribusinesses manage their operations efficiently. It provides tools for tracking crops, livestock, tasks, inventory, and generating insights through a single dashboard.

Who can use SmartFarm?

SmartFarm is designed for smallholder farmers, commercial farms, and agribusiness managers. Whether you're managing a small family farm or a large agricultural operation, SmartFarm can help streamline your management processes.

Is my farm data secure?

Yes, data security is our top priority. All data is encrypted in transit and stored securely. We follow industry best practices to protect your information and comply with data protection regulations.

Can I use SmartFarm on mobile?

Yes! SmartFarm is fully responsive and works on mobile devices, tablets, and desktops. You can access your farm data and manage operations from anywhere with an internet connection.

How do I get started?

Simply click the "Request Access" button and fill out the form. Our team will review your request and contact you to set up your SmartFarm account. Existing clients can log in directly.

What kind of support is available?

We provide comprehensive support through our help documentation, email support, and for enterprise clients, dedicated account management. Check out our Help page for more information.
