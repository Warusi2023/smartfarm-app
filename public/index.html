Frequently Asked Questions

What is SmartFarm Dashboard?

SmartFarm Dashboard is a comprehensive farm management platform designed for mixed livestock and crop operations. It helps farmers track fields, livestock, inventory, finance, and tasks through a unified dashboard to maximize production and profitability.

Who can use SmartFarm Dashboard?

SmartFarm Dashboard is designed for smallholder farmers, commercial farms, and agribusiness managers in Fiji and beyond. Whether you're managing a small family farm or a large agricultural operation, SmartFarm Dashboard helps streamline operations and eliminate middlemen.

Is my farm data secure?

Yes, data security is our top priority. All data is encrypted in transit and stored securely. We follow industry best practices to protect your information. See our Data & Security page for details.

Can I use SmartFarm Dashboard on mobile?

Yes! SmartFarm Dashboard is fully responsive and works on mobile devices, tablets, and desktops. You can access your farm data and manage operations from anywhere. Native mobile apps for Android and iOS are coming soon.

How do I get started?

Click "Request Beta Access" and fill out the form. Our team at Small Ville Company will review your request and contact you to set up your SmartFarm Dashboard account. Existing clients can log in directly.

Who is behind SmartFarm Dashboard?

SmartFarm Dashboard is developed by Small Ville Company, based in Fiji. Our mission is to eradicate poverty by empowering farmers to maximize production, transform products into byproducts, eliminate middlemen, and increase export markets. Learn more.
