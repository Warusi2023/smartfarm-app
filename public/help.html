Help & Documentation

Get started with SmartFarm and learn how to manage your farm operations

Getting Started

How to Log In

  1. Navigate to the Login page
  2. Enter your email address and password
  3. Click "Login" to access your dashboard
  4. If you don't have an account, request access first

First Time Setup

After logging in for the first time:

  • Complete your farm profile with basic information
  • Add your crops and livestock
  • Set up your farm location and boundaries
  • Configure your preferences and notifications

Dashboard Overview

The SmartFarm dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of your farm operations:

Main Dashboard

The main dashboard (dashboard.html) includes:

  • Farm Overview: Quick stats and key metrics
  • Crop Management: Track crops, planting schedules, and harvest data
  • Livestock Management: Monitor livestock health, breeding, and inventory
  • Task Management: Organize and track daily tasks and work orders
  • Inventory: Manage farm supplies, equipment, and resources
  • Analytics: View reports and insights about your farm's performance

Simple Dashboard

The simple dashboard provides a streamlined view for quick access to essential features. Access it at dashboard-simple.html.

Key Features Guide

Crop Management

Track your crops from planting to harvest:

  • Add crop varieties and planting dates
  • Monitor growth stages and health
  • Record harvest data and yields
  • Generate crop reports

Livestock Management

Manage your livestock efficiently:

  • Track individual animals or groups
  • Record health checks and treatments
  • Monitor breeding and reproduction
  • Manage feed and nutrition

Task & Work Orders

Organize your farm operations:

  • Create and assign tasks
  • Set priorities and deadlines
  • Track completion status
  • Generate work order reports

AI Advisory

Get intelligent recommendations:

  • Receive crop and livestock health alerts
  • Get planting and harvesting recommendations
  • Access market insights and pricing information
  • View predictive analytics

Mobile Access

SmartFarm is fully responsive and works on mobile devices:

  • Access your dashboard from any smartphone or tablet
  • View and update farm data on the go
  • Receive notifications and alerts
  • Use location services for field mapping

Tip: Bookmark the dashboard URL on your mobile device for quick access.
